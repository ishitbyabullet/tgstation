18 April 2021

ArcaneDefence updated:

  • Ice cream vats don't needlessly say the an additional time when dispensing cones.
  • Statues can be rotated by alt clicking on them now.

CeUvI updated:

  • Resprited beakers and bottles.

Celotajs updated:

  • Stopped obscenities from appearing on slot machines

Foxtrot (Funce) updated:

  • Bone Gel Recipe works again!

Ghilker updated:

  • bluespace vendor no longer get stuck when the gas you filling with finishes midway

JosephJomama updated:

  • Bounty cubes now pay out the correct speedy delivery bonus.

Y0SH1M4S73R updated:

  • You can now remove people's wigs via the strip menu. As a consequence, doctors can now perform head surgeries on incapacitated wig wearers.

dragomagol updated:

  • Kilostation medbay has been renovated! The security checkpoint has been moved to face the lobby, the old sec checkpoint has been made into an operating theatre, cryogenics and the exam room have traded places, and the delivery office is now a paramedic dispatch!

necromanceranne updated:

  • Armor now checks if an attack is weak against armor, and if so, multiplies any existing armor by two.
  • Hollowpoint bullets and shrapnel now follow this logic, and no longer grant someone additional armor if they have any armor at all.
